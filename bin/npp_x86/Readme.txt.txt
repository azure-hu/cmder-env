Icon for Notepad++ by Piksely

To use the icon in Notepad++ on Windows, follow the steps below

1. Right Click on your Notepad++ icon (shortcut, not the .exe file)
2. Open Properties
3. Click on the Change Icon button in the Shortcut tab
4. Click on Browse
5. Select the "notepad_plus_plus.ico" file
6. Click on OK to close the dialog boxes
